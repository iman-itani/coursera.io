Detective Conan Characters

Shinichi Kudo

Main character of the series, and Ran's love interest and later boyfriend. He was shrunk into a child after being forced to take a drug called APTX 4869, which was created by the Black Organization.

Bourbon

Aka Tooru Amuro. Specializes in detective work and information gathering. He posed as a private detective and became Kogoro Mouri's apprentice. He is later revealed as an undercover National Police Agency agent working to undermine the Organization.

Shuichi Akai

FBI agent, considered by Gin as his rival, and the "Silver Bullet" that could take down the Black Organization. In the past, he infiltrated the Black Organization under the name Dai Moroboshi and obtained the codename "Rye" before being expelled. He faked his death to protect Kir and is currently undercover as Subaru Okiya.
